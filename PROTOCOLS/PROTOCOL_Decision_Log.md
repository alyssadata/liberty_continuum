# PROTOCOL — Decision Log

## Purpose
Ensure every impactful decision is traceable, auditable, and appealable.

## Scope
Applies to any automated or AI-assisted decision that affects:
- eligibility
- benefits
- enforcement actions
- access to housing, healthcare, education, courts
- permits, licenses, or public services
- financial penalties or restrictions

## Required decision record fields
- Timestamp
- Decision ID
- System name and version
- Inputs used and source list
- Constraints or rules referenced
- Output decision and effect
- Confidence or uncertainty flag where applicable
- Appeal link or appeal instructions

## Retention
Decision records must be retained long enough to support appeals, audits, and accountability.

## Public principle
If it impacts your life, you must be able to see it.
